Our Story

The UP2CLOUD Story

Born from years in the trenches of enterprise cloud — UP2CLOUD exists to give every company access to senior-level cloud expertise without the enterprise price tag.

🌱

Founded 2016

Vila Real, Portugal

UP2CLOUD was founded by Cesar A. Nogueira after years building cloud infrastructure for companies like CI&T, everis (NTT Data), Accenture, and ZeroLight. The core idea was simple: enterprise-grade cloud expertise, delivered as a lean, agile consultancy — without the bureaucracy.

🤖

AI-First since 2022

Embracing LLMs before the hype

When AI tools became production-ready, UP2CLOUD pivoted to an AI-first delivery model — embedding AI agents into incident response, cost governance, and pipeline automation. Clients saw 10× faster resolution times and 30–50% cloud cost reductions within the first quarter.

🌍

Going Global

3 continents, remote-first

With offices in Portugal and Brazil and Madrid on the horizon, UP2CLOUD now serves clients across Europe, LATAM, and North America. The APAC Offshore Team service was launched in 2024 to give European companies access to senior engineers at a fraction of the local cost.
