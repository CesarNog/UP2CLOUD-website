# UP2CLOUD LinkedIn Outbound Playbook

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- CTO / Head of Engineering / DevOps Lead
- Companies spending €5k+ monthly on cloud
- SaaS, FinTech, eCommerce, AI startups

## Daily Routine (30–40 min)
- 20 connection requests
- 10 DMs
- 5 follow-ups

## Connection Message
Hi {{firstName}} — quick question.

Are you currently optimizing cloud costs or exploring AI automation in your infrastructure?

## DM 1 (after accept)
Hey — I’ve been helping teams reduce 20–40% cloud spend and automate ops workflows.

Happy to share a quick Cloud & AI audit if useful.

## DM 2 (Day 2)
Quick one — are you currently using AWS, GCP, or Azure?

## DM 3 (Day 4)
Most teams I speak to are overspending 20–50% without visibility.

If helpful, I can run a quick audit and show exactly where savings and automation opportunities are.

## DM 4 (Day 7)
If now’s not the right time, no worries.

But if you want a quick second opinion on your cloud setup, happy to help.

## Goal
- 10 conversations/week
- 3 calls
- 1 closed deal
